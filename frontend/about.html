Our Story

Cooking connects people — across generations, cultures, and kitchens. At E-Recipe Book, our mission is to make cooking simple, joyful, and accessible. Whether you are a beginner or a seasoned chef, our recipes are curated to inspire and guide you. In this modern world everyone depends on internet and mobile phones. Keeping this thing in the mind we designed food recipe android application to assist users to cook like a master. This paper introduces a food recipe app designed to help the users in cooking. This food recipe app aims to improve the cooking skills of user. and quick in comparison to such an application with a locally stored database. In this paper we summarize implementation details and results of the system practice. This system is a proper source for beginner and professionals. Most importantly, we connects home cooks with their greatest sources of inspiration — other home cooks. We're the world's leading digital food brand, and that inspires us to do everything possible to keep our community connected. Sixty-million home cooks deserve no less.
